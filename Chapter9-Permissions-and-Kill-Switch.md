# Chapter 9 — Permissions and the Kill Switch

## Overview

This chapter documents the permission model and the always-available kill switch.

## Revocable Permissions

Every permission granted to the engine (fee routing, order placement) can be revoked independently from the dashboard.

## Kill Switch

The kill switch immediately halts all engine activity for a token: no further fee routing, no further order placement. It is designed to be reachable in one action from the dashboard at all times.
