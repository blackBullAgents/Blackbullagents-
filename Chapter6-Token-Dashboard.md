# Chapter 6 — Token Dashboard

## Overview

The dashboard is the primary interface for monitoring a token once the engine is active.

## Key Widgets

- **Example Bot Wallet** — shows the operating wallet's current balance and recent activity
- **Real-Time Market Health** — spread, depth, and recent price action
- **Fee Routing Summary** — how much has been redirected and over what period

## Reading Market Health

Market health indicators are designed to give a quick operator-level view: is liquidity improving, holding steady, or thinning out relative to trading volume.
